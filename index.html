About Me

I’m Alberto Hipitulan III, the creative mind behind JIDS ART. With over a decade of hands-on experience in graphic design and a background in UX/UI, I help brands bring their stories to life through bold, thoughtful design.

Design isn’t just my job—it’s my passion. Whether it’s a striking logo, an eye-catching social media post, or a full brand identity, I aim to blend creativity with clarity. My design style is clean, clever, and always built with purpose.

I'm also the founder of DotCreate, a creative and IT company specializing in helping startups and businesses level up with strategic visuals, websites, digital and business solutions.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me exploring ideas, learning new design trends, or spending time with my family. My work is a reflection of who I am: passionate, driven, and committed to quality.

Let’s create something awesome together.
